Rules

Harassment, threats, intimidation, or toxic behavior of any kind will result in immediate action. Keep the environment professional.
3. No Spamming
Do not spam messages, emojis, images, reactions, or commands.
4. No NSFW Content
Any explicit, sexual, graphic, or adult content is strictly prohibited.
5. Use Appropriate Language
Profanity, hate speech, slurs, or offensive language is not allowed.

Your Discord username must match your Roblox username.
7. Limit Mentions

8. No Advertising
No advertising other servers or services.
9. No Alternate Accounts

11. No Bypassing Rules

12. No Drama

13. Keep Content Relevant
Stay on-topic in channels.
14. No Impersonation

15. Use Common Sense

16. Zero Tolerance Policy
Serious violations may result in immediate ban.
