Server Rules
1. Respect Everyone
Treat all members with courtesy and respect at all times. Disrespect, sarcasm meant to insult, or rude behavior is not allowed.

Harassment, threats, intimidation, or toxic behavior of any kind will result in immediate action.
3. No Spamming
Do not spam messages, emojis, images, reactions, or commands.
4. No NSFW Content
Any explicit, sexual, graphic, or adult content is strictly prohibited.
5. Use Appropriate Language
Profanity, hate speech, slurs, or offensive language is not allowed.
6. Username Requirement
Your Discord username must match your Roblox username.
7. Limit Mentions
Do not spam @mentions.
8. No Advertising
No advertising other servers or services.
9. No Alts
Only one account allowed.
10. Follow Staff Instructions
Staff decisions are final.
11. No Bypassing Rules
Bypassing rules in any form is still a violation.
12. No Drama
No arguments or drama in chat.
13. Stay Relevant
Keep chats on-topic.
14. No Impersonation
No impersonating staff or users.
15. Use Common Sense
If it seems wrong, don’t do it.
16. Zero Tolerance
Serious violations may result in instant ban.
